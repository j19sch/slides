three core capabilities

internal locus of control

external scope of considering

personal source of agency

accountability others initiative locus of control: degree to which we experience and exercise control over our own lives, particularly on the direction of our self-development and our reslience to adversity spectrum: external (our lives determined by others vs taking accountability of outcomes and direction) scope of considering: what we take into account in our actions and endeavors, especially in relation to other people and living beings (self-centered vs systems-actualizing) spectrum: only ourselves versus others in our world and other forms of life source of agency: where we find authority for our initiative and actions (authority of others to direct us vs within us the will to initiate action and follow through) spectrum: authoritarian worldview versus stepping up and making a difference Or Jocko Willink: extreme ownership, cover & move, extreme ownership / default aggressive / get after it
