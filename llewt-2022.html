Theme:
Testing beyond the testers

Agile testing has been around for a long time now, yet testers predominantly handle testing towards the end of the Software Delivery Life Cycle and are held responsible for quality.

Many people have successfully introduced testing activities earlier and coached development teams to carry out test activities.

We want to hear your experiences of success and failure in shifting testing left, enabling teams to consider testing throughout and encouraging the whole team to take ownership of quality.
