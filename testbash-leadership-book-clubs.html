why do a book club?

  • discuss books
  • encouragement to read
  • discover new books
  • connect with different people
  • people got promoted (correlation != causation)
  • excuse to buy more books
  • connect (aspiring) leaders, change agency in company, people self-select for leadership (Esther Derby)
  • break from regular work, time to reflect

how to do a book club?

  • create a space to gather in
  • pick a clear theme
  • announce the new book early and widely
  • gently steer conversations
  • space: Miro, Slack
  • about leadership not about management
  • decide new book well in advance
  • announcing -> new members
  • during discussion keep an eye on not straying too far from the book
  • lots of company venting at the start
  • keep on track but also allow diverging
